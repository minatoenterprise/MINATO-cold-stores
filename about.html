Where Freshness Docks

Our Story

The name Minaato nods to the Japanese word Minato (港), meaning "harbor" or "port"—a trusted place where goods arrive and are kept safe. Our brand expresses that same idea for Afari: a reliable harbor where freshness docks and stays fresh for your kitchen.

We operate with modern deep freezers and a careful cold chain to keep frozen fish and chicken safe and satisfying. Whether you’re a household, vendor, or small restaurant, we serve with honesty and consistent quality.

Our Values

  • Trust: Clear prices and reliable storage.
  • Freshness: Consistent cold chain from freezer to you.
  • Service: Friendly ordering via cart or WhatsApp.
  • Community: Proudly serving Afari and nearby areas.

Local business. Community focused.
